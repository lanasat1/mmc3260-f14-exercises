Siesta between classes at the University Auditorium, with a nice view.

The Auditorium offers a calm relaxing atmosphere to catch a few Zzz's before heading off to library west or your next class. Featuring three floors of a padded benches, window walls, you can find really get in some solid power napping; and if you're lucky the orchestra occasionally plays as background music.
